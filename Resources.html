How BudgetPlanner Ensures Data Security

We use encryption techniques to secure data both in transit and at rest. This means that when data is being transmitted between the user's device and the app's servers, or when it's stored on servers, it's encrypted and therefore difficult for unauthorized parties to access. Secure login methods, such as passwords, PINs, or biometric authentication (like fingerprint or face recognition), ensure that only authorized users can access their accounts.
