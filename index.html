What is Budget Planner, and how does it work?

Knowledge is power. You have to know your finances to master them. Budget Planner puts all your money in one app, under your control, wherever you may be. Sync automatically with your financial accounts to predict and plan for a richer life, while saving money every day.

All in One Place

Budget Planner can connect with over 15,000 banks and finance providers, giving you a single overview of all your money.

AI Empowered Categories

Budget Planner automatically categorizes and labels transactions, making your spending easy to organize.

Stats: The Big Picture

High-level visibility into your current and predicted financial status gives you the chance to lead a richer life.
